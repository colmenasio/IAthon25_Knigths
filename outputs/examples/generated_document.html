Analysis Report on JAMB Score Dataset

Overview

This report summarizes the analysis performed on a dataset containing various educational factors and their impact on students' JAMB scores. The dataset includes variables such as JAMB score, study hours, attendance rate, teacher quality, school type, and other relevant factors.

Dataset Description

The dataset comprises the following columns:

  • JAMB_Score: The score obtained by the student in the JAMB exam.
  • Study_Hours_Per_Week: The number of hours the student spends studying each week.
  • Attendance_Rate: The percentage of classes attended by the student.
  • Teacher_Quality: A rating of the quality of teachers.
  • Distance_To_School: The distance from the student's home to their school (in kilometers).
  • School_Type: The type of school (e.g., Public or Private).
  • School_Location: The location of the school (e.g., Urban or Rural).
  • Extra_Tutorials: Participation in extra tutorials (Yes/No).
  • Access_To_Learning_Materials: Access to essential learning materials (Yes/No).
  • Parent_Involvement: Level of parental involvement (Low, Medium, High).
  • IT_Knowledge: Student's level of IT knowledge (Low, Medium, High).
  • Student_ID: A unique identifier for each student.
  • Age: Age of the student in years.
  • Gender: Gender of the student (Male, Female).
  • Socioeconomic_Status: Socioeconomic status of the household (Low, Medium, High).
  • Parent_Education_Level: Highest education level achieved by the parents (None, Primary, Secondary, Tertiary).
  • Assignments_Completed: Number of assignments completed by the student.

Gender and JAMB Score Analysis

  • A t-test was conducted to analyze the relationship between Gender and JAMB_Score:

python male_scores = df[df['Gender'] == 'Male']['JAMB_Score'] female_scores = df[df['Gender'] == 'Female']['JAMB_Score'] t_stat_gender, p_value_gender = stats.ttest_ind(male_scores, female_scores)

  • Results:
  • T-statistic: t_stat_gender
  • P-value: p_value_gender

The results of the t-test indicate whether there is a significant difference between the JAMB scores of male and female students.

School Type and JAMB Score Analysis

  • An ANOVA test was performed to analyze the effect of School_Type on JAMB_Score:

python public_scores = df[df['School_Type'] == 'Public']['JAMB_Score'] private_scores = df[df['School_Type'] == 'Private']['JAMB_Score'] f_stat_school_type, p_value_school_type = stats.f_oneway(public_scores, private_scores)

  • Results:
  • F-statistic: f_stat_school_type
  • P-value: p_value_school_type

The results from the ANOVA test provide insights on whether the type of school (Public vs. Private) has a statistically significant effect on JAMB scores.
